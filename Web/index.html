Error 405

Request rejected by server

Http405:Error

This is the error screen to notify that the server has rejected your request. This error is very common and can arise for several reasons, the most common is that your IP is registered in the server's blacklist, its solution is to remove your VPN or proxy if you have it, it can also be because of your firewall, so disable your firewall. It may also be that the server has Anti-DDoS/DoS activated so it may be rejecting all requests or is under a denial of service (DoS) attack or a distributed denial of service (DDoS) attack. What we recommend is to write an email to

sergiocaseroverdial@gmail.com

and report the situation. Another possibility is that your internet provider or your wifi network blocks the web, although it is unlikely, your request may have been manipulated so that the server rejects it, it may be by a hacker in your network, your internet provider or the administrator of your network. For this case it is better to contact your service provider or restart the router, you can also try to fix the error yourself, enter the router administrator's website, usually these two links:

192.168.1.1
